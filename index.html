Welcome to our page!
We are two friends who turned our passion for crafting into a business venture.
Let us tell you our story..

Our mission is simple: to deliver quality, precision, and attention to beautiful details.

We, Ayaa and Salma, met during our college years. Initially, Salma would design while I would embroider, creating personalized gifts for our friends and family.
The joy it brought to them sparked an idea among our peers to start a small business together.
In 2021, we took the leap and founded our Facebook and Instagram pages. Since then, our journey has been nothing short of amazing.


Crafting isn't just a hobby for us; it's a labor of love. We pour our hearts into every piece, ensuring that it carries a piece of our passion to its recipient.
Seeing the smiles on their faces and knowing that we contributed to their happiness fills us with immense joy.
Whether it's a gift for a celebration or a token of appreciation, we feel honored to be part of our customers' special moments.
